Ambition

I'm Lavleen, a 15-year-old based in Hong Kong with a deep passion for photography and visual storytelling. My camera is how I make sense of the world: finding beauty in fleeting moments and turning them into something timeless.

Photography started as a hobby and quickly became something I can't imagine living without. From learning editing techniques to experimenting with genres: concert, sport, portrait, documentary. I'm constantly growing and ready to take the next step in a professional studio environment.

Beyond photography, I'm an active public speaker and dedicated volunteer, both of which have sharpened my communication, empathy, and eye for the human story behind every shot.

Beyond the Camera

Photography

From composition to post-processing, I'm constantly learning and experimenting across concert halls, sports venues, theatres and classrooms. Photography is where my technical curiosity and creative vision meet. I aspire to turn it into a career.

Public Speaking

I love the challenge of thinking on my feet and communicating with purpose. Public speaking has built my confidence and sharpened how I connect ideas, skills that translate directly into telling stories through the lens.

Volunteering

Giving back to my community is something I genuinely believe in. Volunteering keeps me grounded, empathetic, and motivated, and it reminds me that the most powerful images are the ones that connect us to each other.
